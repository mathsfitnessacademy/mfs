Practice on Our Tool

Maths Fitness Academy provides an environment to practice mathematical calculations online. This tool also gives you a detailed analysis of your performance during your course time.

Apart from our weekly sessions, this tool brings you multiple questions based on the weekly lesson. Answering these questions will make you faster and accurate in calculations.

How it works!

Login / Register
Reach login page by clicking Login / Register button showed in website.
Create account
You can create an account by clicking on new user if you are a new user.
Login to the account
Existing students can login to the account using username and password.
First Assessment
After account creation is complete, you have to attend an assessment test.
Practices
You can start daily practice after you mentor instructions.
Analysis
You can analyze your performance of daily practice on our website.
Final Assessment
A finall assessment will take place after your course completion.
Check Improvement
You can have a detailed view on your overall peformance in our tool.
Download Certificate
You can download your certificate after the entire duration of the course.

Student Evaluation

There will be a assessment test at the beginning of the course. Student have to attend this test to evaluate ability before the course.
Another assessment test will be conducted at the end of the course and exam take place after the entire duration of the course.
A comparison dashboard between both assessment will be available for students to analyze the improvement of the student.

Daily Practice

Attend daily practice on our tool to do your excercises. The questions appear on tool are completely based on weekly lessons provided by trainer.
You can analyze your daily performance on our tool. The details of each questions you attended will be available for you immediately after completion.
A dashboard will be available for you to assess your improvement during the course. The dashboard will give you a summary of your course performance.

You can continue practice by purchasing extended practice.
